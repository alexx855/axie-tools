# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Report security-sensitive issues privately.
- Avoid sharing private keys, tokens, seed phrases, or other secrets in issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Posting secrets, credentials, or instructions intended to steal assets.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after maintainers ask for the thread to return to the issue.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other moderation actions when needed to protect the project and its contributors.
